About our project

We had the idea to make this project first to welcome Erasmus students, but we decided to extend it later to every student who would like to grow fresh crops at their place.

This project aims to help students to get fresh, suitable and sustainable foods while they get some skills at growing specific seeds according to specific conditions.

It is a means to gather students together, to make them discover one anothers culture, bring some laughs, memories and create warm moments between students while sharing a tool, a pinch of leaves, degusting a recipe or just watering together their crops.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make student life easier, healthier, and more sustainable. We believe that growing your own food shouldn’t be complicated or expensive, it should be simple, accessible, and rewarding.

By offering affordable seeds and gifting one to international students, we help young people save money, eat better, and reconnect with nature.

The Team

Blau Arbiol, Web Developer

Blau brings the project to life online. She designs and builds the entire website from the inside out, crafting the structure, the code, and the experience that connects our seeds with students around the world.

Her work makes sure everything runs smoothly, beautifully, and sustainably.

Florent Hien, Seed Science Expert

Florent is the scientific heart of the project. He studies each plant carefully, its growth needs, nutritional value, and environmental impact, to ensure every seed we offer is efficient, healthy, and perfect for students’ daily life.

Thanks to him, every seed is more than just a plant: it’s smart science in action.

Duna Estrada, Graphic Designer

Duna gives our project its visual identity. She designs the packaging and graphics that make each seed feel special, combining creativity, simplicity, and eco-friendly design. She makes sure our products are as we envision.

Her touch turns every pack into a story about growth, care, and connection.

Our Collaborators

We proudly work with Lithuanian partners who support sustainable agriculture, education, and innovative growing solutions for students.

AgroTTC contributes professional knowledge in seeds, cultivation, and agricultural training. Their work helps us ensure students receive seeds that are reliable, high-quality and suited for urban environments.

AUGA Group is one of Lithuania’s leading leaders in sustainable organic farming. Their commitment to eco-friendly production inspires our mission to help students grow food that is clean, affordable, and planet-friendly.

Lettuce Seeds

0.50€

Lettuce is fresh, crispy, and packed with vitamins. It grows fast, so you can harvest it several times a season. It likes cooler weather and plenty of water. Perfect for students, you can even grow it in a box or recycled container!

Steps to grow Lettuce:
1. Buy your seed, a pot between 10-25cm and garden soil or compost
2. Put the soil/compost in the soil, add 2/3 seeds
3. ⁠Make sure to have a temperature between 10-20 degrees. You can use Led grow light or just expose to sun
4. ⁠Watering each 2/3 days with 200-250ml of water but make sure not to have a soggy soil
5. ⁠Take care of the leaves, the pot and the environment
6. ⁠Harvest after 3/6 weeks

  • Quantity: 10 Seeds
  • Recomended Temperature: 15-25C°
  • Water: 1 or 2 glasses
  • Germination Time: 2 days

About our project

We created this project to make student life a little easier and greener. By growing your own food from a single seed, you can save money, eat fresh, and enjoy the satisfaction of cultivating something yourself. Our goal is simple: to help students everywhere access affordable, sustainable food.

programmes for foreign students

University events

We organize activities with discussions, debates, seminars, documentaries. We share moments in which we can all discuss and discover the world of agriculture and green innovations.

Student discounts

As we want to encourage students to afford our seeds which contribute to funds we gather to give to associations, researchers, and NGOs, we offer 20% discounts for students having ISIC cards.

Making it easy

Once a month we organize a practical session where we show you how to sow, harvest and take care of your seeds. We also take time to answer questions and share tips to have a good experience.
